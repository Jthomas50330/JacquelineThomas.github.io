Niagara Falls

Niagara Falls is located in both New York and Canada. I had always wanted to go there ever since I saw them in the "Superman" movie.


A Barrel of Fun

As you can see, my aunt and I had a wonderful time. It was a hot day, so we decided to cool off going over the falls. My sister dared us to do it!

We Survived!

We survived to tell a story, and I survived to do some coding about it.

A Story it Was!

Just kidding! This was a souvenir pic. It was nice to feel the mist on a humid day in New York.

Maid of the Mist

My favorite part of the falls was riding on the Maid of the Mist. We got to see Falls close up. It was an awesome experience!

Canada

I recommend staying on the Canadian side if you like to do the touristy stuff. They have lots of restaurants and shops.
